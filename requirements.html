Application Requirements

Everything you need to know to apply for the Master of Management Program at Chulalongkorn Business School

Checklist

Required Documents

Prepare the following documents before starting your application. All documents must be submitted online within the deadline.

description
1

Official Transcript

Official transcript of previous university in any field. No minimum GPA required, but the date of graduation must be shown.

badge
2

Identification Document

A copy of your citizen identification card or passport showing applicant's name, date of birth, and expiry date (for foreigners).

translate
3

English Proficiency

Minimum score of one of the following English proficiency tests:

check_circle CU-TEP: 75
check_circle TOEFL iBT: 79
check_circle IELTS: 6.5
recommend
4

Recommendation Letters

Two letters of recommendation from present or former instructors or employers.

download Download Recommendation Form
work_history
5

Work Experience

Applicants must have at least one year of work experience. Those with less than one year need to show equivalent experience or related activities.

cloud_upload
6

Online Submission

All documents must be submitted online within the deadline. Late submissions will not be accepted.

Important Notes

Remarks & Exemptions

schedule

English Score Validity

English proficiency scores are valid if they are no more than two years old (referring to the test date until Fall semester starts).

verified

English Exemption

English proficiency score will be exempted for:

  • check_circle Citizens of USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand
  • check_circle Those who completed a degree from institutions in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Ireland, Hong Kong, Philippines, Singapore, India, and South Africa where teaching is entirely in English
