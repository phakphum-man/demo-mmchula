Foundation Program

Announcement for Foundation Program

When you are accepted to the MM program, you will be advised of the foundation program. The foundation program is required for ALL students entering the MM program to study all 5 principles-level courses in management, marketing, accounting, finance and economics.

Completion ensures that all students come into the MM program with the same foundation needed to start the MM courses.

Enrollment Confirmation
